Music

General Information

There’s something about music that makes people think it is an inherent skill. Tell someone to try pottery, and they’ll give it a try despite never having done it before. And yet, do the exact same thing with music, and they’ll be quick to assure you that they don’t have the ‘knack’ for it.

This is, however, not accurate. Seldom are there people with ‘natural’ musical talent - and even rarer are those who’ve become famous musicians. Musical performance is a learned skill, something that anyone is capable of if they’re willing to put in the effort.

The piano is the core instrument of modern music, and for good reason - it has the widest range of any instrument. Image credit: rpcschoolofrock.org
The violin is a popular starting instrument, as well. It can be either a classical or a folk instrument. Image credit: fineartamerica.com
