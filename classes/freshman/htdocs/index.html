About Me

My name is Grey Patterson, and I am a freshman at Linfield College. I am pursuing a double-major in computer science and music, and I hope to have a career in the field of “making stuff.”

I enjoy photography, and took the two photos visible in this page, titled "Beach" and "Path," respectively. Some of my other photos have been linked to from this page, and the rest are available on my personal website and blog, a link to which is available below.

I also, as demonstrated by this entirely hand-coded site, enjoy working with both the technical and the graphic aspects of design, and hope to be able to incorporate those elements into my career.

Following my graduation from Linfield College, I plan to start a career in the computer science industry, although I have yet to choose a specific focus for that venture.
